GroupEM Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how GroupEM, a Chrome extension, collects, uses, and discloses your information.

Information We Collect

GroupEM uses Google Analytics to collect anonymized data about your usage of the extension. This data may include:

  • The pages you visit within the extension
  • The features you use
  • How long you use the extension
  • Any errors you encounter

This data is collected solely to improve the functionality of GroupEM and to understand how users interact with the extension. We do not collect any personal information that can be used to identify you.

Use of Information

The information collected through Google Analytics is used for the following purposes:

  • To improve the design and functionality of GroupEM
  • To identify and fix bugs
  • To understand how users interact with the extension

Disclosure of Information

We may share anonymized data collected through Google Analytics with third-party service providers who help us operate and improve GroupEM. These service providers are contractually obligated to keep your information confidential and to use it only for the purposes for which it was disclosed.

Your Choices

You can opt out of having your browsing activity within GroupEM tracked by Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on: here.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The updated privacy policy will be posted on the GroupEM website. We encourage you to review this privacy policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
